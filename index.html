What's Superbytes Podcast?

Superbytes podcast is dedicated to tools, technology and projects or really anything we've picked up within the last few weeks working in the web development field. Our goal is to say something that will be beneficial for others working as developers and especially helpful to people trying to break into web development as a career.

Brought to you a few times a month by Jordan Austin and Cy Klassen. Never miss an episode, subscribe to the Superbytes feed.
